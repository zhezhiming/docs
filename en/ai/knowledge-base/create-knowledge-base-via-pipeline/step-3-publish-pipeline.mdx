After you have finished orchestrating and debugging your knowledge pipeline, you can officially publish it.
In XpertAI, **a Knowledge Pipeline is an extension of a Digital Expert's capabilities**, so its publishing process is identical to that of a Digital Expert.

Click the **"Publish"** button in the upper right corner of the page. Enter a release note in the popup and confirm to successfully publish the knowledge pipeline.
