**Copilot Command** is a command mode for AI Copilot in the Xpert AI platform. Users can directly control the functionality of the current page by entering specific commands. The AI assistant will parse the user's input, identify tasks, and call the relevant agents and tool functions to perform logical operations. Whether querying data, triggering page actions, or executing complex tasks, Copilot Command can quickly respond to and fulfill the user's requests, improving work efficiency and simplifying processes.

## General Commands

- `/clear` Clear the session  
- `/help` or `/?` View help information

## Examples

**Copilot Command** retrieves command examples configured in the backend through its **FewShot** capability, helping the AI model better understand and execute user instructions. FewShot technology allows the AI to infer based on examples of similar tasks, providing more accurate feedback and operations when executing complex tasks. This mechanism enables the AI to quickly adapt and complete assigned tasks using existing command examples, without requiring extensive training, thus improving execution efficiency and accuracy.
