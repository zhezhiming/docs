Pie charts are circles divided into multiple regions or slices. Each slice represents a count or percentage of observations for one level of a variable.

## Pie Chart

Pie charts require one primary dimension and one measure.

![Small](./pie-attributes.png)

![Small Shadow](./pie.png)

### Doughnut Chart

By selecting the pie chart's variant property, you can set the chart effect to a doughnut chart.

![Small Shadow](./doughnut.png)

### Nightingale Chart

By selecting the pie chart's variant property, you can set the chart effect to a Nightingale chart.

![Small Shadow](./nightingale.png)

## Trellis

Like other charts, pie charts can also display as multiple chart grids by setting a trellis dimension.

![Middle Shadow](./trellis.png)

As shown below, by adding a calendar dimension and setting its role to **Trellis**.

![Small](./trellis-chart-annotation.png)
