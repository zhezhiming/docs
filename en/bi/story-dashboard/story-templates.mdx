The story dashboard template feature refers to a function provided in the metrics management system for quickly creating and customizing dashboards. It allows users to quickly build their own dashboards based on predefined templates or template libraries to display key business metrics and data visualization charts.

The main features and functions of the dashboard template feature are as follows:

1. **Quick Start**: Dashboard templates offer a series of predefined layouts, metrics, and chart styles, allowing users to quickly start the dashboard creation process without designing from scratch. Users can choose templates that suit their business needs, saving time and effort in designing and building dashboards from the ground up.

2. **Standardization and Consistency**: Dashboard templates ensure that dashboards used throughout the organization have a consistent appearance and functionality. By using unified templates, standardized dashboard designs can be established, making dashboards across different users or departments have similar interfaces and user experiences, enhancing user consistency and comprehensibility.

3. **Customizability**: The dashboard template feature usually has a certain degree of customizability, allowing users to adjust and modify according to their own needs. Users can customize the selection of metrics, configuration of data sources, styles of charts, and arrangement methods to meet their specific business requirements.

4. **Best Practices and Recommendations**: Dashboard templates are often based on best practices and industry standards, providing feasible dashboard design solutions and recommendations. These templates are carefully crafted by designers and professionals to offer the best data visualization and user experience, helping users quickly establish effective dashboards.

5. **Accelerated Analysis and Decision-Making**: The purpose of dashboard templates is to help users perform data analysis and business decision-making more quickly. Through predefined metrics and charts, users can quickly obtain key information, discover trends and patterns, and make decisions based on this data, accelerating business actions and responses.

## Creation

Story template attributes:

* **Name**: The name of the story template, used for identification and search in the template library.
* **Description**: The description of the story template, used to describe the main content and purpose of the template.
* **Type**: The type of story template, used to distinguish between pure **theme** and layout **template**.
* **Public**: The public attribute of the story template, used to control the visibility of the template.
* **Tags**: The tags of the story template, used for categorizing and organizing the template.
* **Preview Image**: The preview image of the story template, used for display in the template library.

## Template Library

The dashboard template library stores both user-created and system-public dashboard templates. Users can quickly find the templates they need through the search box or tag filter.

![Story Template Library](/public/img/story/story-templates.png)

Clicking the apply button on a template can apply the template to the current story.

In the **My** template library, users can delete templates they have created.
