# Business Domain

## Overview

Business domains are used to categorize and manage enterprise business, organizing related business functions together for easier management and permission control.

## Features

- **Business Classification**: Categorize enterprise business
- **Domain Management**: Create and manage business domains
- **Permission Control**: Permission control based on business domains
- **Resource Organization**: Organize business-related resources

## Creating Business Domains

### Steps

1. Navigate to the Enterprise Organization page
2. Click the "Business Domain" menu
3. Click the "New Business Domain" button
4. Fill in business domain information
   - Business domain name
   - Business domain code
   - Business domain description
5. Configure business domain settings
6. Save business domain

## Business Domain Management

### Business Domain List

- View all business domains
- Search business domains
- Filter business domains
- Sort business domains

### Business Domain Configuration

- **Basic Information**: Modify business domain name, description, etc.
- **Member Management**: Manage business domain members
- **Permission Settings**: Configure business domain permissions
- **Resource Allocation**: Allocate business domain resources
