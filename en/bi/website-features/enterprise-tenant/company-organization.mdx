# Company Organization

## Overview

Company organization functionality is used to manage enterprise organizational structures, including departments, teams, positions, and other hierarchical structures.

## Organizational Structure

### Hierarchy

Supports multi-level organizational structures:
- Company
- Department
- Team
- Position

### Organization Types

- **Department**: Departments under the enterprise
- **Team**: Teams under departments
- **Group**: Groups under teams

## Management Functions

### Creating Organizations

1. Select parent organization
2. Click "New Organization"
3. Fill in organization information
4. Save organization

### Organization Information

- **Organization Name**: Display name of the organization
- **Organization Code**: Unique identifier of the organization
- **Parent Organization**: Parent organization it belongs to
- **Organization Head**: Head of the organization
