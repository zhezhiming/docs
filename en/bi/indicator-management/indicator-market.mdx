The indicator market is an important part of the indicator management system. Its main function is to provide a platform for users to publish, search, share, and subscribe to indicators.

The indicator market usually includes the following functions:

* **Indicator Publishing:** Allows users to publish indicators they have developed and make them available for use by other users.
* **Indicator Search:** Allows users to search and browse indicators to find the right ones.
* **Indicator Sharing:** Allows users to share indicators with other users for collaborative research and analysis.
* **Indicator Subscription:** Allows users to apply to subscribe to indicators to obtain the data they need.

**Benefits**

Through the indicator market, users can search and subscribe to indicators online, and they can also perform analysis online. This can improve work efficiency and save time costs.

## How to Use

You can use the filter conditions on the left side of the page to find the business area you need to browse. The query conditions of the indicator market are as follows:

* **Type:** Basic indicator, derived indicator
* **Tag:** A list of tags for all indicators. You can select multiple tags to filter indicators.
* **Business Area:** System business area, with business area hierarchy and the number of corresponding subordinate indicators. After selecting a business area, you can browse the indicator information in this business area on the right side.

Click the **Apply** button on the indicator to apply for data permissions. You can also click **Apply for Business Area** to apply for authorization for all indicators within the business.

The **Search** box in the title bar can be used to search for indicators globally. The number of indicators after filtering will be displayed on the business area hierarchy.

Click the **Indicator Name** to go to the indicator details page for viewing.

## Approval Apply

On the approval page of indicator management in the [Project](../../stories/project/), users can manage indicator-related applications under the current project and approve or reject related applications. After the approval is passed, the applicant can view the indicator in the indicator application. If it is the business area of the applied indicator, the business area administrator will operate on the indicator approval page in their default project.
